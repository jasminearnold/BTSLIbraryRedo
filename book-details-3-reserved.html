The midnight library

Haig, Matt, 1975- Author.

 

Book/Text | Viking | 2020

 

The Midnight Library ponders the infinite possibilities of life. It is about a young woman name Nora Seed, who lives a monotonous, ordinary life and feels unwanted and unaccomplished. One night, her despair reaches a peak and she commits suicide.
